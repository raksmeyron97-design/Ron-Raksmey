About Me

Hello! I'm Ron Raksmey

I am an aspiring and enthusiastic educator, currently serving as a Year 2 Student Teacher (គរុនិស្សិត) in primary education (12+4 system) at Phnom Penh Teacher Education College (PTEC) and pursuing a Bachelor of Mathematics in Year 3. I am deeply passionate about making foundational learning and mathematics accessible and enjoyable for primary school students.

My approach to teaching blends theoretical knowledge with modern tools like AI, LaTeX, and Canva to simplify complex concepts. I strongly believe in creating personalized learning plans that help young learners build confidence and improve their overall academic performance from an early age.

I am preparing to officially become a Bachelor-level primary school teacher in 2028, and I am actively seeking roles where I can contribute to student growth and early academic excellence.
